Pepperoni pizza

The larger-than-life slice we all know and love is surprisingly easy to make at home from scratch.

Ingrediants

  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package regular active or fast-acting dry yeast (2 1/4 teaspoons)
  • pepperoni
  1. flatten dough into a circle
  2. apply tomato sauce
  3. add cheese
  4. top off with pepperoni
