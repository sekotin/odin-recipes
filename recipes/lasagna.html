Pan Lasagna

Cheese Filling Mix the ricotta and cheeses (per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl, and set aside. No ricotta? No problem, cottage cheese works just fine in this recipe! Meat Sauce Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes. Layer Together Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling

Ingrediants

  • ricotta cheese
  • meat sauce
  • noodles

Steps

  1. Mix the ricotta and cheeses
  2. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste
  3. Layer Together Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles
  4. bake until bubbling
